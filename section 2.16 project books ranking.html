My top 3 books of all time.

A Personal History by Imran Khan

This book is written by Imran Khan himself and provides a detailed account of his life, his cricket career, his philanthropic efforts, and his political journey.

The Cricketer, The Celebrity, The Politician by Christopher Sandford

This biography offers an in-depth look at Imran Khan's life, covering his achievements in cricket, his personal life, and his transformation into a political leader.

The Victory of Imran Khan's Cricketing Knights by John Ward

This book details Pakistan's 1992 Cricket World Cup victory under the captaincy of Imran Khan, highlighting his leadership and strategic acumen.
